"Ecological Oasis" is an innovative initiative aimed at creating a unique ecological environment in urban space. The project envisages the arrangement of a special zone in the city, where nature and technology are harmoniously combined.

Key elements of the project include the creation of vertical gardens that enclose the grounds, the integration of solar panels and the use of renewable energy sources to provide electricity. It is also planned to install a system for collecting and cleaning rainwater for watering plants.

This project seeks to combine modern technology and nature to create an aesthetically pleasing and livable environment.
